CONTACT US

At RainyDays, we're always here to help you on your journey. Whether you have questions about our products, need assistance with an order, or just want advice on picking the perfect hiking jacket, our team is ready to assist.

Feel free to reach out through any of the following methods:

  • Email: For general inquiries or order support, email us at support@rainydays.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

  • Phone: Need immediate assistance? Call us at 1-800-RAINYDAY. Our customer service team is available Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 5 PM (GMT).

  • Store Locations: Prefer to shop in person? Visit us at one of our physical stores! Check our Store Locator for the nearest RainyDays location.

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you're reaching out for support or just want to share your latest outdoor adventure, RainyDays is here to help you stay prepared for your next journey.
