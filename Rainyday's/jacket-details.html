PILGRIM ANORAK JACKET

We've designed the Pilgrim jacket for demanding outdoor activities in dynamically changing weather. Based on the zippered "Kangaroo" construction proven for decades, it has been refined to meet the expectations of modern travelers, while still retaining the aesthetics of the "old guard."

Numerous pockets allow convenient placement of equipment, while their arrangement keeps them accessible, even when kneeling. The whole construction is designed for full upper body coverage, regardless of the equipment you carry.

By lowering the "kangaroo" pocket, we were able to extend the front zipper, allowing better ventilation during strenuous activities.
