Made from durable, weather-resistant materials, it typically offers insulation, breathability, and water resistance or waterproofing. Features often include a hood, adjustable cuffs, and multiple pockets for storage.

Whether you're battling rain, wind, or cold, a quality outdoor jacket ensures comfort and protection, allowing you to enjoy your adventure regardless of the elements
